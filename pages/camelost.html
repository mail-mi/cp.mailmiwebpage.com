Camelost

Series: The Final Four, book 1.

Main Characters:

  • Seth Robinson: An 18-year-old basketball player who finds out he's heir to the throne of Camelot.
  • Maddy Robinson: Seth's sister. A 15-year-old actress-to-be who gets kidnapped by King Mordecai of Cornwall and forced to work as a kitchen slave
  • Skyler Hunter: Seth's friend. A 17-year-old history and photography buff who also gets kidnapped by King Mordecai and is told that he is the heir to Camelot's throne.

Back-of-book blurb: On Seth's 18th birthday, he receives a pocket watch that takes him, his friend Skyler, and his sister Maddy back in time to Camelot, where Seth learns he's the heir to King Arthur's throne. Skyler and Maddy are kidnapped by King Mordecai, the soveriegn ruler of the rival kingdom of Cornwall, and Seth needs to rescue them before they can get back home. Will he be able to mount a rescue party before a brewing war breaks out?

Current Status: Draft 4 in progress.
