About Me

Hi, I'm Liam!

I'm Liam, and I'm a beginning novelist (that's a fancy term for "author who hasn't been published yet"). I'm currently working on my first series, The Final Four, but you can read more about some of those books elsewhere on this website, because you came to this page to learn about me!

I'm attending college right now at BYU in Provo, Utah. I'm majoring in Mathematics, with minors in Computer Science and Creative Writing. I enjoy writing, playing Pokemon, and eating too many desserts. I'm married to the lovely woman in the photo, Hadley Ashton, and she is pregnant with our first. Little Maren Grace Ashton will be here in November!

You can find me on LinkedIn, on GitHub, or on Facebook.
